How to find your forever friend

Assembly time: 2 hours
Awesome rating: 52% awesome

Are you curious how someone finds a connection with their pet? Learn how to find your forever friend today!

  1. Financial Stability

    It is iportant to be sure you are financially prepared to take on the responsibility of a dog.

  2. old or young

    Remember,your forever friend may not be a puppy. But, old dogs need love too.

  3. I choose you.

    It's important to keep in mind most of the time the dog will choose you, rathe than you choose the dog.

  4. Training your puppy

    Potty taining can be the most difficult part about having a puppy.

  5. Providing a Loving Home. A forever home, for your forever friend.

    Having a loving home can build the trust between the owner and the dog. Having an unstable home can make it hard for your new friend to adjust. Be sure there's plenty of space for the dog you plan to take home.
